Interstellar¶

Instances of Interstellar are objects representing model interstellar processes which modify source intensities.

class xpsi.Interstellar.Interstellar(*args, **kwargs)[source]¶

Bases: xpsi.ParameterSubspace.ParameterSubspace

Base class for model interstellar X-ray processes (e.g., absorption).

__call__(energies, pulse)[source]¶

Subclass to write a specialised specific flux modifier.

Parameters:
  • energies – A numpy.ndarray of energies in keV at which the specific fluxes in pulse are calculated.
  • pulse – A numpy.ndarray of specific fluxes, with energy increasing along rows and phase increasing along columns.
Returns:

None.

Note

It is expected that the operations performed on a column of specific fluxes need to be applied identically to all other columns. The referenced object pulse needs to be directly modified, and not copied.
